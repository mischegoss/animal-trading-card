Judgmental Chihuahua

A Chihuahua is the world's smallest dog breed

  • Name Origin: Chihuahua, Mexico
  • Alias: Arizona' and 'Texas' Dogs
  • Coat: Long and Short Coat
  • Habitat: Blankets and Laps

Small in size but big in personality, the Chihuahua is truly one of a kind. They are energetic, affectionate, playful and fiercely loyal
